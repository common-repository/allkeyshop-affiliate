Allkeyshop Widget integration for WordPress

== Description ==
[Allkeyshop.com](https://www.allkeyshop.com) is a website to compare price of digital video games with more than 3 million users every month.
Our affiliate program allows you to earn money for clicks your users make on a personalized and non-intrusive widget placed on your website.

Everything is easy to use, the tools are designed to be customized to fit the look and content of your site.


To use this plugin, you must have an account on the [Allkeyshop Affiliate website](https://affiliate.allkeyshop.com/).
